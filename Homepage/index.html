Bio

 

I am a first year Ph.D. student of Computer Science at University of California, Berkeley. I am working on the LoCal.

 

I received bachelor degree of Computer Science and Technology from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). During my undergraduate, I spent six months at Carnegie Mellon University Silicon Valley (CMUSV) as a visiting student working with Prof. Pei Zhang. I was working on SensorFly and PANDAA at CMUSV. After that, I joined the Mobile and Sensing Systems group at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), working with my research mentor Dr. Chieh-Jan Mike Liang. I was involved in LiveSynergy and a large-scale RFID-based asset-tracking system.

 

I have two full papers published in UbiComp 2011 and IPSN 2012. And I also won two Best Demo Awards at UbiComp 2011 and SenSys 2011 respectively.
